The Department of Information and Communications Technology (DICT) - Sorsogon Provincial Office plays a vital role in promoting digital transformation, innovation, and public service within the province. As an intern from January to May 2025, I was given the opportunity to be part of various technical, administrative, and outreach initiatives aimed at strengthening digital literacy and government service delivery. Through the guidance of my mentors and the support of the DICT team, I was able to deepen my skills in programming, networking, system testing, robotics, documentation, and public speaking. This experience not only enhanced my technical capabilities but also taught me the importance of professionalism, teamwork, and continuous learning in a real-world environment. I am truly grateful for the chance to contribute to the organization's mission and to grow both personally and professionally during my time at DICT Sorsogon.

DICT - Sorsogon

January - May 2025 | DICT - Sorsogon Provincial Office, Burabod, Sorsogon City

Certifications & Courses

I completed courses under Cisco including Basic Computer Skills, Ethical Hacker, Python Essentials 1, and Data Analytics Essentials. Through these, I gained certificates and digital badges that recognize my foundational and technical skills.

Programming & Web Development

I developed a Virtual CV using HTML and CSS, showcasing my front-end development skills. Additionally, I created basic programs using MakeCode, further enhancing my understanding of coding logic and visual programming.

Networking & System Testing

I took part in assisting participants during the eLGU System Testing Implementation in Prieto Diaz and Irosin, Sorsogon. We helped the Local Government Units (LGUs) of these municipalities understand how to properly use the eLGU system and how it functions for their governance needs.

Mobile Robotics

Initially, I participated in building and programming the Matrix Micro:bit Car. Later, I had the opportunity to be the discussant for the Matrix Micro:bit Car topic, guiding immersion students through their activities and explaining the basics and applications of robotics using the Matrix Micro:bit Car.

Technical Documentation & Reporting

I served as an Officer of the Day multiple times, recording daily activities, and preparing logs. I also created After-Activity Reports (AARs) after events and sessions, maintained my own Daily Time Records (DTRs) and accomplishment reports, and was involved in proofreading and updating technical documents for internal reporting.

Public Speaking & Presentations

I had the opportunity to discuss the Philippine National Public Key Infrastructure (PNPKI) to my fellow interns as part of DICT's Digital Literacy Program. I also led a robotics discussion where I introduced and explained the Matrix Micro:bit Car to immersion students.
