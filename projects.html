PiliTrade Online Marketplace

PiliTrade Online Marketplace is a capstone project designed to recognize the economic potential of the Pili industry. By leveraging digital technology, the system connects local sellers with buyers, promoting economic growth and expanding market reach.

Key Features

Marketplace

Connects local sellers with buyers to promote Pili products.

Easy Registration

Simple onboarding process for sellers and buyers.

Messaging

Enable communication between sellers and buyers.
