PiliTrade Online Marketplace

PiliTrade Online Marketplace is a full-stack capstone project that I had the opportunity to design and develop, handling both the Frontend and Backend aspects. It aims to recognize and maximize the economic potential of the Pili industry by leveraging digital technology to connect local sellers with buyers, promoting economic growth and expanding the market reach of Pili-based products.

Technologies & Languages Used

Frontend

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Bootstrap

Backend

PHP (Laravel Framework)

Database

MySQL

Other Tools

Livewire, Postman, GitHub

Project Evaluation

The PiliTrade project was successfully presented to the panel, where we demonstrated its key features and functionalities. The feedback we received was largely positive, with valuable suggestions and recommendations for further improvement. We were fortunate to receive strong support and positive evaluations, and we appreciated the constructive feedback that will help us refine the project further.

Final System Evaluation Grade

1.3

Key Features

Online Marketplace

Connects local sellers with buyers to promote and sell Pili products efficiently.

User Registration & Profiles

Easy registration process and personalized profiles for Sellers and Buyers.

Buyer-Seller Messaging

Real-time messaging feature to enable seamless communication between users.

Product Management

Sellers can add, update, and manage their products with detailed information and images.

Product Browsing & Filtering

Buyers can easily browse, search, and filter products by categories and subcategories.

Mobile Responsive

Fully responsive design for smooth access on smartphones, tablets, and desktops.
